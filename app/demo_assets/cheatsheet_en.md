# SHAPERS Quick Study Cheat-sheet (English)

## Top Prompts
- "Suggest a weekly study plan for Class 10 Punjab board exams, focusing on Maths and Science."
- "How can I improve my marks in Mathematics in 30 days?"
- "Give a 7-day revision plan for Physics Class 12 (CBSE)."
- "Which chapters are high-weightage for Class 10 PSEB Maths?"

## Study Plan Template
- Morning (1.5 hr): New topic study
- Afternoon (1 hr): Practice problems
- Evening (45 min): Revision & flashcards
- Sunday: Weekly test (2 hrs)

## Exam Checklist
- Keep original admit card and 2 ID proofs
- Revision notes, formula sheet, stationery
- Sleep 7-8 hours before exam

## Punjab-specific Tips
- Follow PSEB syllabus and previous-year papers from official site
- Balance Punjabi medium and English medium terms when reading NCERT
